About Me

With over 10 years of experience in the field of Psychology, I work as a Clinical Psychologist and Neuropsychologist, providing specialized care based on scientific evidence.

Currently, I am a PhD Candidate in Neuroscience and Cognition, developing advanced research that integrates knowledge from cognitive neuroscience with clinical practice. I also work as a University Professor, sharing knowledge and training new professionals committed to excellence.

My approach combines scientific rigor, empathy, and dedication to human development, both in clinical practice and in academia.

Services

Psychotherapy

Individual psychological care based on contemporary approaches, focused on self-knowledge, emotional development, and resolution of mental health issues.

Neuropsychological Assessment

Detailed assessment of cognitive functions (memory, attention, language, executive functions) for differential diagnosis and planning of personalized interventions.

Professional Supervision

Clinical supervision for psychologists in training or those who wish to improve their practices, focusing on clinical cases, ethical aspects, and continuous professional development.
