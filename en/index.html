About me

I am a psychologist and neuropsychologist with clinical and academic practice, working with individuals across the lifespan.

My work is grounded in scientific evidence and centered on individual experience, promoting ethical and effective mental health care.

I also work as a researcher and university lecturer, integrating clinical practice, research, and education.

Services

Neuropsychological Assessment

What it is

A detailed investigation process that seeks to understand the relationship between brain and behavior through interviews, observation and specific instruments, assessing functions such as memory, attention, language, executive functions and reasoning.

What it's for

  • Describe cognitive, affective and behavioral functioning;
  • Identify therapeutic needs and recommend interventions;
  • Contribute to differential diagnosis and multidisciplinary collaboration.

Psychotherapy

A safe space for care, transformation and self-knowledge.

What it is

A collaborative and structured process focused on emotional health care, with evidence-based interventions.

How it works

In a space of listening and welcome, we will work together to understand your story. The goal is to identify what has been preventing your well-being and create practical strategies for the changes you seek.

Who it's for

Recommended for those who want to address emotional issues such as anxiety, stress and depression, and also for those who want to better prepare for life's challenges. The therapeutic process is an investment in yourself. Mental health is for everyone!

Clinical Supervision in Neuropsychological Assessment

Who it's for

Psychologists and students who wish to improve their practice with technical safety and scientific rigor.

What we cover

Formulation of hypotheses, selection of instruments, data interpretation and preparation of reports, assessments and expert opinions.

Research Consulting

Methodological and statistical support for research projects in psychology and neuroscience.
